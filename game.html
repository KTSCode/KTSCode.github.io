Controls

Interact With People and Objects - “i”

Life Forms

Life forms are not directly controllable. Instead they are located around the ship, waiting for the AI to interact with them.

Drones

Drones are directly controlled by the AI. They can be instructed to interact with objects and life forms in the environment.

Doors

The AI should be able to open or close any door on the ship.

Drone Bay

Starting position and resting place for the drone.

You must be in The Drone Bay in order to access a list of your Objectives

Med Bay

Activate/Deactivate the healing pods.

Bridge

Take evasive maneuvers.

Generator

Control general power levels on the ship.

AI Core

Where Larry is located.

Crew Room

Where the crew sleeps and keeps journals on personal computers.
