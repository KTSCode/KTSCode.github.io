Basic Mechanics

Room Control

Clicking on a room that the AI has control of should display all options currently available for that room.

Life Forms

Life forms are not directly controllable. Instead they are located around the ship, waiting for the AI to interact with them.

Drones

Drones are directly controlled by the AI. They can be instructed to interact with objects and life forms in the environment.

Drone Functionality - Hacking Allows the AI to physically interface with systems that are on a closed network.

General Room Mechanics

Air Level

The oxygen levels of specific rooms is adjustable. Low oxygen levels are potentially fatal.

Temperature

The temperature of specific rooms is adjustable. Temperatures too cold or hot are potentially fatal.

Lights

Lights can be turned on and off for any specific room. Rooms without light may make it difficult for life forms to see.

Doors

The AI should be able to open or close any door on the ship.

Room Specific Mechanics

Shields

Change power level of the shields.

Engines

Change ship speed and activate warp drive.

Oxygen

Change the oxygen levels.

Weapon Control

Fire and aim weapons.

Drone Bay

Starting position and resting place for the drone.

Med Bay

Activate/Deactivate the healing pods.

Bridge

Take evasive maneuvers.

Sensors

Get information about enemy ships and handle cyberwarfare.

Generator

Control general power levels on the ship.

AI Core

Where Larry is located.

Crew Room

Where the crew sleeps and keeps journals on personal computers.
